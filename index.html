Mobile app connecting directly with parking systems and smart city sensors to show open parking spots in real time.

Step 1
Find
See real-time open spots on the map with 100% accurate data.
Step 2
Claim
Tap to book your spot instantly before you arrive.
Step 3
Block
The spot turns red for everyone else so nobody can take it from you.
